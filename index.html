Yang Liang

Welcome to my website! I am an applied microeconomist with research interests in public policy analysis across the fields of labor economics, health economics, and international economics. I’ve been an assistant professor of economics at San Diego State University since 2018 and assistant director for the Center for Health Economics and Policy Studies since 2021.
